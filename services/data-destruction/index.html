OneGuard Services

Certified Data & Hardware Destruction

Secure end-of-life disposal with full chain of custody documentation.

Overview

When hardware reaches the end of its life, the data it holds remains a risk until it is properly destroyed. OneGuard provides certified decommissioning and physical destruction of hard drives, SSDs, and other storage media — giving you documented proof that your data has been irreversibly eliminated. We handle the full process with strict chain of custody controls.

Enquire About This Service

What We Offer

  • Certified data wiping to DoD 5220.22-M and NIST 800-88 standards
  • Physical shredding and destruction of hard drives and SSDs
  • Destruction of USB drives, magnetic tapes, optical media, and mobile devices
  • Full chain of custody documentation throughout the process
  • Certificate of destruction issued for every engagement
  • On-site and off-site destruction options available
  • Compliant disposal in line with data protection regulations
